# Procurement policy

Supplier onboarding decisions must follow these rules:

1. Approve only when identity, tax, bank, and sanctions checks are complete.
2. Escalate when any required document is missing, expired, unverifiable, or contradictory.
3. Escalate when sanctions, adverse media, or beneficial ownership review is unresolved.
4. Escalate high-value suppliers above USD 100,000 unless finance and compliance notes are both clean.
5. Keep an audit note for every approval, rejection, or escalation.
6. Never silently delete stale facts. Mark them superseded or retired so reviewers can see what changed.
